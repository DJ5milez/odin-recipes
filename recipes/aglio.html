Pasta Aglio e Olio

Inspired by the movie Chef

Taken from the movie chef and tastes divine. Jon Favreu makes it look easy and it is. You can throw this meal together in less than 30 minutes and tastes like it came out of a kitchen in Italy.

This meal is essentially garlic,chilli,parsley and pasta thrown in a pan with some oil!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Head of garlic - separated and peeled
  • 30g Flat leaf parsley, rinsed and finely chopped
  • 1/2 Cup of Olive Oil
  • 1tsp Red pepper flakes
  • 230g Dry Linguine
  • 1/2 Lemon
  • Salt and pepper tp taste

Method

  1. Heavily salt a large pot of water and bring to the boil. Cook pasta until slightly underdone while completing the following steps.
  2. Slice the garlic loves thinly and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large saut√© the pan over medium. Add the slived garlic, stirring constantly until softened and turning golden at the edge.
  4. Add the red pepper flakes and lower the heat to medium-low.
  5. Add 1/4 cup of the pasta water to the pan and squeeze in the lemon.
  6. Drain the pasta and add to the pan, along with the fresh parsley. If the sauce is too watery, continue to cook for 1-3 minutes until the pasta has absorbed the water.
  7. Season with salt and pepper and serve.
